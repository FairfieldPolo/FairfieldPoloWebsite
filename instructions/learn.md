# Learn (Polo 101)

The **Learn** section (`/learn`) lists **Education articles** from Sanity. Each article has its own URL: `/learn/[slug]`.

## Adding or editing an article

1. In Studio, open **Education article** (or create new).
2. **Title** — shown as the page heading.
3. **URL slug** — generate from the title; this becomes the last part of the link.
4. **Category** — rules, equipment, history, glossary, or how to watch (used for grouping/filtering on the learn index).
5. **Level** — beginner, intermediate, or advanced.
6. **Excerpt / intro** — short teaser on the listing page.
7. **Article body** — main content (paragraphs, headings, images with captions).
8. **Cover image** — optional; add **Alt text** for screen readers.
9. **Published date** — used for ordering and display.

Publish when ready. New articles appear on `/learn` after the usual short cache delay.

## Removing an article

Unpublish or delete the document in Studio. Broken links to old URLs may still exist elsewhere—update those links if you shared them publicly.
